# Strategex AI Function Images

This directory should contain 9 images for the business strategy functions.

## Required Images

Place the following 9 images in this directory:

1. **diagnosis.png** - Business Diagnosis & Problem Identification
2. **market.png** - Market & Competitive Analysis
3. **planning.png** - Strategic Planning & Growth Strategy
4. **financial.png** - Financial Strategy & Profitability
5. **gtm.png** - Go-To-Market Strategy
6. **operations.png** - Operations & Process Optimization
7. **risk.png** - Risk Management & Business Continuity
8. **leadership.png** - Leadership / Board-Level Decision Support
9. **execution.png** - Execution Roadmap & KPI Planning

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Dimensions**: 400x400px or similar square ratio
- **Style**: Icons or illustrations representing each function
- **Background**: Transparent or solid color

## Fallback

If images are not found, the system will display a placeholder with the function name.
